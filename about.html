About Checkpoint

Checkpoint is a page designed to help prospective students of NACOMES (National Association of Computer Engineering Student), YABATECH, that are undergoing their various academic programmes to help boost their knowledge on various course being offered in the pursuit of both "National Diploma and Higher National Diploma" in Computer Engineering. Checkpoint provides links to past questions and solutions to different courses ranging from first and second semester's of every years past Examination offered in Computer Engineering, YABATECH
